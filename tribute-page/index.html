Keith Jarrett

A man who has touched the soul of the piano

Keith Jarrett performing at one of his concerts

    Life between the exit signs:

  • 1945 - Born on May 8, 1945 in Allentown, Pensylvania
  • 1963 - Moved to Boston where attended Berklee College of Music
  • 1967 - "Life between the exit signs" - his first album as a leader - was released
  • 1969-1970 - Played with Miles Davis' group
  • 1970 - Recorded solo piano album "Facing you"
  • 1975 - Recorded "The Koln concert", one of his famous masterpieces
  • 1987 - Jarrett's 100th solo performace took place in Japan...
